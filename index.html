CitrusEye Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your CitrusEye account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  • Contact Support: Send an email to support@citruseye.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.
  • Verification: Our support team will verify your request and identity.
  • Confirmation: Once verified, we will delete your account and all associated data, including personal information and images captured through the app.

Data Retention:

  • Personal information, images, and other data associated with your account will be permanently deleted.
